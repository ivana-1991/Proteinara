The Process

From protein to performance

1

Protein Blend

We combine whole-grain flours with high-quality protein and fresh cottage cheese, creating a nutrient-dense base in small batches.

2

Stone Bake & Drying

Each loaf is baked on stone, then gently dried in the oven to remove excess moisture — creating a firmer structure, richer texture, and naturally extending freshness without additives.

3

Fresh Delivery & Storage

Because we use real cottage cheese, our bread is naturally perishable. Keep it refrigerated to maintain freshness, extend shelf life, and preserve its full nutritional value.
